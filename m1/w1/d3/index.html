Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new holiday starter!

featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-porpouse flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, through it's not necessary
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
